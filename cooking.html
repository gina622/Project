Beef with Broccoli

Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flank Steak 1.25 lbs
Vegatable Oil 1 tablespoon + 1 teaspoon
Broccoli 2 cups
Minced Ginger 2 teaspoon
Minced Garlic 1 teaspoon
Oyster Sauce 1/4 cup
Beef Broth/Water 1/4 cup
Sugar 1 teaspoon
Sesame Oil 2 teaspoons
Soy Sauce 1 teaspoon
Cornstarch 1 teaspoon
Salt and Pepper To taste
Instructions
  1. Heat 1 teaspoon of oil in pan over medium heat. Add broccoli and cook for 4 min.
  2. Add the ginger and garlic to the pan and cook for 30 sec more.
  3. Remove the broccoli from the pan; place on plate and cover
  4. Wipe pan clean and turn heat to high. Add remaining tbsp of oil
  5. Season steak pieces with salt and pepper and add them to pan in a single layer. Cook for 3-4 min on each side
  6. Add broccoli mixture back to pan and cook for 2 more min.
  7. In a bowl whisk together oyster sauce, beef broth, sugar, sesame oil, and soy sauce. In a small bowl, mix cornstarch with a tbsp of cold water
  8. Pour oyster sauce mixture over beef and vegatables; cook for 30 sec. Add cornstarch and bring to boil; cook for 1 more minute.
  9. Serve immediately, with rice if desired.

Honey Garlic Salmon

Time: 20 min
Servings 4
Ingredients Quantity
4 Salmon fillets 4-6 oz each
Salt and Pepper To taste
Apple Cider Vinegar/ Lemon Juice 2 teaspoon
Olive Oil 1 tablespoon
Minced Garlic 1 1/2 teaspoon
Chopped Parsley 1 tablespoon
Butter 3 tablespoons
Instructions
  1. Heat olive oil in large skillet over medium high heat. Season salmon fillets with salt and pepper.
  2. Place salmon fillets, skin side up. Cook undisturbed for 5 min.
  3. Carefully flip salmon and cook for additional 5-6 min.
  4. Remove salmon from pan. Add butter to pan and melt
  5. Add garlic to pan and cook for 30 sec, stirring constantly. Stir in lemon juice and salt and pepper.
  6. Sprinkle parsley over salmon, then serve.

Teriyaki Chicken

Time: 20 min
Servings 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken Breasts, diced cubes 1 1/4 pounds
Olive Oil 1 tablespoon
Soy Sauce 1/4 cup
Water 1/4 cup
Honey 2 tablespoon
Light Brown Sugar 1 1/2 tablespoon
Rice Vinegar 1 tablespoon
Sesame Oil 1/4 teaspoon
Minced Ginger 2 teaspoon
Minced Garlic 2 cloves
Cornstarch 2 teaspoons
Sesame seeds and green onions For Serving
Instructions
  1. Heat olive oil in skillet over medium high heat
  2. Add chicken and let browm on bottom about 3-4 min then flip and continue to cook 3 min longer
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, water, honey, brown sugar, vinegar, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, and cornstarch.
  4. Stir sauce in cooked chicken and continue to cook until sauce thickens (30-60 sec)
  5. Serve warm garnished with green onions and sesame seeds if desired.

Avocado Toast with Egg

Time: 10 min
Servings 1
Ingredients Quantity
Multi Grain Bread 1 slice
Avocado 1/2 whole
Lemon Juice To taste
Egg 1 whole
Butter 2 teaspoon
Salt and Pepper To taste
Instructions
  1. Toast bread in toaster
  2. Cut avocado and remove seed
  3. Scoop out avocado into bowl, add lemon juice, pinch of salt and pepper.
  4. Smash avocado with fork to texture you like
  5. Spread onto toasted bread
  6. Top with cooked eggs

Garlic Shrimp Pasta

Time: 15 min
Servings 4
Ingredients Quantity
Linguine Pasta 8 oz
Olive Oil 1 tablespoon
Raw Shrimp 1 pound
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Black Pepper 1/4 teaspoon
Butter 1 tablespoon
Minced Garlic 1 clove
Parmesan 1/2 cup
Parsley 1/4 cup
Instructions
  1. Add pasta to boiled salt water and cook until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water and drain pasta.
  2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat and add olive oil. Cook shrimp for a minute, then season with salt and peper.
  3. Continue to cook shrimp until pink, remove onto plate and set aside
  4. In same pan, melt butter and add garlic. Cook for 30 sec.
  5. Add parmesan, let it melt then loosen the sauce with reserved pasta water and simmer for 1 min.
  6. Toss the pasta with the sauce until well coated. Add shrimp back in to reheat, garnish with parsley and serve.

Beef Buritos

Time: 45 min
Servings 6
Ingredients Quantity
Ground Beed 1 pound
Chopped Onion 1/2 cup
Minced Garlic 3 cloves
Tomato Paste 3 teaspoons
Dried Oregano 1 teaspoon
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Lime Juice 2 tablespoons
Water 1/4 cup
Rice 2 cups
Refried Beands 15 oz
Shredded Cheddar Cheese 2 cups
Tortillas 8 large 10-inch
Instructions
  1. In skillet, over medium high heat, add ground beef, onion, and garlic. Cook and crumble beef and cook until no longer pink. Drain grease.
  2. Add tomato paste, oregano, salt, lime juice, and water to beef. Cook and stir until slight boil. Reduce heat to low and cook for 5-8 min.
  3. Remove from heat to low and let stand for 15 min
  4. Lay tortillas flat and divide beans, cheese, beef, rice among tortillas. Fold burrito-style.

Spaghetti

Time: 11 min
Servings 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 12 oz
Tomato Sauce 15 oz
Olive Oil 2 tablespoon
Minced Garlic 5 cloves
Salt 2.5 teaspoon
Black Pepper To taste
Oregano 2 teaspoon
Parmesan Cheese To taste
Instructions
  1. Fill pot with cold water 3/4 full and bring to a boil. Add 2 tsp salt and spaghetti.
  2. Separate pasta a few times during first 2 min of cooking.
  3. Cook pasta uncovered for 5 more minutes/ until al dente, stir occasionally. Drain spaghetti.
  4. Return pot to me and add olive oil, 4 garlic cloves and oregano. Stir for 20 seconds.
  5. Add drained pasta, tomato sauce, remaining 1/2 tsp salk, black pepper and 1 garlic clove.
  6. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and serve.
